Job se bore hone lagte hain tab bahar nikalnek liye unhe 3 raste dikhte he MBA, IAS or STARTUP ". Well true it is. Most of the people during their college life think about an idea and about how life changing that idea is but most of them also give up on their ideas as the path to becoming an entrepreneur is not that easy. Entrepreneur is a person who converts his ideas into a real business and wantrepreneur is the one whose ideas remain as just ideas. We all must have felt that feeling of excitement whenever we watch TVF 's Pitchers or the very famous Silicon Valley, for that idea but we all must have also felt those sparks fading away and the main reason for that is the question which we ask to ourselves "Is my idea one in a million?". 90% of the start-ups fail and as you are reading the article probably 40 new start-ups were created across the world and 85 which existed are shut down! But it is also a fact that ever since Mark Zuckerberg hit the jackpot with Facebook, many young entrepreneurs have come up with great innovations and successful start-up businesses. Starting up a start-up is notachieved without stumbling with a few rocks on the road but as it is truly said that it all starts with just an idea. The best ideas normally emanate from yourown experiences and your immediate surroundings. Look at your idea on along-term basis. Will your idea be able to run on in long term? If so, then you are on the right path.

There is a very famous quote from one of the web series "Jab is desh ke graduates apni 9-5 The second most important thing when building a start-up is Seed funding and we all have an advantage here as colleges provide numerous opportunities to get funding for our dream ideas. It is required to analyze the start-up and expenditure well. Students can also participate in various Hackathons and Ideathons or in various other events or they can just present their business ideas at BPlan competitions like Eureka (IIT Bombay) or Empresario (IITKGP) etc. As soon as the product is launched in the market, they can pitch for higher level funding. Apart from idea and funding it is important to secure your intellectual-property in which basically a company has their own right to create a product. Examples include patents, copyrights etc, which gives company a differentiating factor. Branding and social media marketing is also equally important as it is required that your product has an impact on people 's minds. One of the best way to set your start-up is to write up a business plan on the basis of your market research. Building a website or an app for your product is the new way to enter into the start-up market. While the entrepreneur may have big dreams, it is ultimately the team whose support he/she requires to reach the goals hence hiring the right team is extremely important and most crucial step for start-up. At the end what 's most important is never losing that spark for your business because you never lose in business, either you win or you learn.
